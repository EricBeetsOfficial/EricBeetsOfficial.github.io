Minsar Studio by Opuscope

Minsar Studio is the immersive editor to prototype and design
interactive 3D/AR/VR scenes, code-free.

The Minsar platform exists to help you prototype an AR/VR app or experience:

  • Create your first scene. You can add up to 50 of them in the same project.
  • Choose an anchor to specify how your content should be placed in the physical or virtual world.
  • Import as many assets as you like, either created by our or your team, or retrieved from providers like Sketchfab.
  • Adjust their position, scale and rotation with incredible precision in real-time using your immersive device. What you see is exactly what your users will get!
  • Use our no-code features to easily add storytelling to your scenes.
  • Date: Decembre 2019-2022
  • Category: Augmented, Mixed, and Virtual Reality

Mappn by Opuscope

Mappn is a social map that allows you to have stronger connections between
your friends, your favorite spots, and your communities!

Mappn is a location-based social media app that allows users to see their friends' locations and activities, as well as post their own Moments (photos or updates) on a map.

The app also has an augmented reality feature for users who would like to see their posts in AR.

‍Mappn aims to bring together users' lives, friends, and favorite spots in one app.
